Transit Access and Equity

Mapping access to public transit in King, Pierce and Snohomish Counties in Washington State.
More information regarding Washington Transit Service studies.
Transit Service levels used in the data are defined by this study and determines the frequency of transit

Project Objective

Understand the level of transit access for census tracts within King county, Pierce County, and Snohomish County, in Washington State
Transit Access Score was calculated using the percentage of area covered by a quarter mile buffer on each Bus stop and the amount of stops per tract
